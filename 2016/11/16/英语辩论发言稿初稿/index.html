英语辩论发言稿初稿

发表于 2016-11-16 |

英语课的pre轮到我这变成了英语辩论。小组讨论后确定我担任的是四辩的角色。
这是一个很好的锻炼机会。以下是我写的四辩稿。

Thanks for the chairman, please let me give my best wishes to everyone here. Now, I will give you the conclusion of this debate.

In the very beginning of this debate, our first debater SQY gives us a very clear definition about naked marriage which is getting a wedding certificate without car, house, ring, and wedding. Then the second debater of our side ZS、the third debater YS list a large number of convincing reasons to argue against naked wedding. They argues from the angle of woman and man, from individual to family and to our society. With no doubt, our statements are really very logical.

But the other side always distort our intention. We never said __. What we want to express is that_. we do not deny ___. Instead,_.　

Reviewing all the debate，it’s not difficult for us to find that there are three important errors my opposite debaters had made.

  • First of all，The most common point held by you is that__ But there is no convincing argument can support it. Perhaps you ignored to analysis the topic of our debate. What we are discussing is whether it is reasonable for us to get a naked wedding not__.

  • Second, If I understood you correctly, you said that __. which I’m afraid you are wrong there because_.

  • Third, you are always escape from our questions, we asked you that___, but during the debate，you always want to avoid direct answer by saying that_

Thus, we conclude that our other sides’ argument is questionable and we insist our point that naked wedding is not reasonable and will do harm to couple‘s relationship.

That’s all for this. Thank you for your listening !
