Title

Twitter Hot Event Detection and Correlation Analysis with the Stock Market

Main idea

How to detect events and topics from Twitter, and whether there are correlations between those topics and the stock market

Content

Detect hot events from tweets

1.Select burst tweet words that might represent hot events.

kurtosis——（峰值系数）（四阶中心矩）

  • In statistics, kurtosis is a measure that can be used to evaluate the “peakedness” or “flatness” of a sequence of data obtained from a real-valued random variable. We use kurtosis specifically to measure the burst degree of each tweet word in a period.
  • 如果一个词的kurtosis超过了给定的阈值则它就是爆炸词（阈值也是通过一系列数学公式确定的而不是随意选取）
  • 已知了爆炸词之后，我们计算爆炸期. We separately find the closest non-burst value at its left and right, denoted as rkj_l and rkj_r. Then, the interval [tj_l, tj_r] is called a burst interval.
  • 将所有在爆炸期内出现与爆炸词相关的tweets聚类（用Jaccard Coefficient计算相关性）
  • 合并相似的类

2.Extract representative words for each event

POS_TextRank

  • POS_TextRank is a text ranking algorithm, we use it to detect representative words for natural language texts
  • TextRank——graph G(V,E) is constructed for a text, in which node set V represents words, and edge set E represents co-occurrence relationships between the words within a window. For each node vi V, its score, S(vi) , indicates its importance.
  • POS_TextRank是对以上方法的改进：incorporate part-of-Speech information into the score computing process by setting different values for different part-of-speech words
